When you need support, be assured that we are continuously monitoring our [Slack channel](https://join.slack.com/t/kubeshark/shared_invite/zt-3jdcdgxdv-1qNkhBh9c6CFoE7bSPkpBQ). Feel free to [open a GitHub issue](https://github.com/kubeshark/kubeshark/issues), contact us through our [online form](https://kubeshark.com/support), or send an email to support@kubeshark.com. We will do our best to respond promptly.
